About Us

Sweet Bites Bakery was founded by John and Jane Jameson, a husband and wife team who share a passion for baking. With over 20 years of experience in the industry, they decided to open their own bakery to share their love of baking with the local community.

At Sweet Bites Bakery, we believe that the key to making great baked goods is using the best ingredients. That's why we only use high-quality, locally sourced ingredients in all of our products.

Our Bakery Items

Bread

Our artisanal bread is a testament to time-honored baking traditions. Made with the finest ingredients and baked to perfection, each loaf boasts a golden crust and a fluffy, flavorful interior that will elevate every meal.

Cake

Indulge in pure bliss with our mouthwatering cakes. From classic flavors to innovative creations, our cakes are masterfully crafted to bring joy to your celebrations. With every moist and decadent slice, you'll savor the love and care put into each delightful creation.

Cookies

Crisp at the edges, chewy at the center—our cookies are the epitome of comfort. These little bites of happiness come in a delightful array of flavors, from traditional chocolate chip to unique combinations that will leave you reaching for more.

Croissants

Transport yourself to a Parisian café with our buttery and flaky croissants. Baked to perfection, each bite offers a heavenly taste and a delightful crunch that pairs perfectly with your morning coffee or afternoon tea.

Donuts

Indulge your sweet cravings with our delectable donuts. Glazed, filled, or sprinkled, each donut is a burst of flavor and a joyous treat that will brighten your day with every delightful bite.

Muffins

Start your day on a delightful note with our scrumptious muffins. Bursting with fresh fruits, nuts, or chocolate chips, these moist and fluffy delights are the perfect on-the-go breakfast or anytime snack

Pastries

Indulge your senses with an array of delicate pastries that are as beautiful as they are delicious. From flaky croissants, buttery danishes, and luscious turnovers to airy puff pastries filled with delightful surprises, our pastry selection will transport you to a pastry paradise.

Pies

Each bite of our handmade pies is a taste of home-baked goodness. Sink your teeth into our fruit-filled wonders, like tangy apple, sweet cherry, and succulent blueberry pies. And for the savory enthusiasts, our savory pies and quiches are the perfect balance of rich fillings and buttery crusts.

Rolls

Our bakery takes pride in crafting soft, pillowy rolls that are perfect for every occasion. Whether you're enjoying a cozy breakfast at home or need a side to complement a hearty dinner, our assortment of dinner rolls, cinnamon rolls, and specialty buns will leave you feeling satisfied and content.

Brownies

A celebration of chocolate awaits you with our heavenly brownies. Rich, fudgy, and utterly indulgent, these decadent treats will satisfy your sweet cravings like no other. Choose from classic chocolate, walnut-studded delights, or try our seasonal variations for a delightful surprise.
